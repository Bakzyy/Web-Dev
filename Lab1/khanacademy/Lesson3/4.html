Recipe #1

  • Time: 2h
  • Serves: 1000tg
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 2 cup
Beef 1 pound
Onion 2
Carrot 3
Oil 1/2 cup
Paprika 1 teaspoon
Zira 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

  • Rinse the rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  • Chop the onion finely.
  • Peel and julienne the carrots.
  • Cube the lamb or beef.

Step 2: Sauté Onions

  • Heat the vegetable oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pot
  • Dutch oven over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent.

Step 3: Brown the Meat

  • Add the cubed meat to the pot and brown it on all sides.

Step 4: Add Spices

  • Sprinkle cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper over the meat. Mix well.

Step 5: Add Vegetables

  • Add julienned carrots to the pot and stir everything together.

Step 6: Add Rice

  • AAdd the rinsed rice to the pot and stir to coat the rice with the mixture.

Step 7: Add Water or Broth

  • Pour hot water or broth over the rice and meat mixture. The liquid should be about 1-2 inches above the rice.

Step 8: Place Whole Garlic

  • Place the whole, peeled head of garlic in the center of the pot. This will add a subtle garlic flavor to the dish.

Step 9: Bring to a Boil

  • Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid.

Step 10: Simmer

  • Let the plov simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.

Step 11: Let it Rest

  • Turn off the heat and let the plov rest for an additional 10 minutes with the lid on.

Step 12: Fluff and Serve

  • Gently fluff the rice with a fork, mixing in the meat, vegetables, and garlic.
  • Serve hot, and you can garnish with fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley if desired.

Source: Uzbek Plov (One Pot Recipe)

Recipe #2 - Classic Pasta

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 250g
Olive Oil 2 tablespoons
Garlic 3 cloves, minced
Tomatoes 3, diced
Basil 1/4 cup, chopped
Parmesan Cheese 1/2 cup, grated
Salt and Pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook Pasta

  • Boil water in a large pot.
  • Add a pinch of salt to the boiling water.
  • Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook until al dente.
  • Drain the pasta and set it aside.

Step 2: Sauté Garlic in Olive Oil

  • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add finely chopped or minced garlic to the hot oil.
  • Sauté the garlic until it becomes fragrant and slightly golden.

Step 3: Add Tomatoes and Basil

  • Dice fresh tomatoes or use canned diced tomatoes.
  • Add the tomatoes to the pan with sautéed garlic.
  • Tear fresh basil leaves and add them to the pan.

Step 4: Season with Salt and Pepper

  • Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste over the tomato and basil mixture.
  • Stir the ingredients to ensure even seasoning.

Step 5: Toss in Cooked Pasta

  • Transfer the cooked pasta to the pan with the tomato and basil mixture.
  • Toss the pasta in the sauce until it is well-coated.

Step 6: Top with Parmesan Cheese

  • Grate Parmesan cheese over the pasta.
  • Optionally, add more fresh basil for garnish.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy!

  • Plate the pasta in individual servings.
  • Optionally, add an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top.
  • Serve the pasta hot and enjoy your delicious meal!

Recipe #3 - Vegetable Soup

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Carrots 3, sliced
Celery 3 stalks, chopped
Onion 1, diced
Garlic 3 cloves, minced
Potatoes 2, diced
Vegetable Broth 6 cups
Tomato Paste 2 tablespoons
Italian Seasoning 1 teaspoon
Salt and Pepper To taste
Spinach 2 cups

Step 1: Sauté Vegetables

  • Heat a pot and add a small amount of oil.
  • Add diced vegetables (e.g., onions, carrots, celery) to the pot.
  • Sauté the vegetables until they are tender and slightly caramelized.

Step 2: Add Broth, Tomato Paste, and Seasonings

  • Pour vegetable or chicken broth into the pot with sautéed vegetables.
  • Add a spoonful of tomato paste for depth of flavor.
  • Season the soup with herbs and spices like thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper.

Step 3: Simmer Until Vegetables are Tender

  • Bring the soup to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and let it simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  • Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking.

Step 4: Add Spinach

  • Wash and chop fresh spinach.
  • Add the spinach to the soup and stir until wilted.

Step 5: Adjust Seasonings

  • Taste the soup and adjust the seasonings as needed.
  • Add more salt, pepper, or herbs to achieve the desired flavor.

Step 6: Serve Hot

  • Ladle the hot soup into bowls.
  • Optionally, garnish with fresh herbs or a dollop of sour cream.
  • Serve the vegetable soup hot and enjoy!

Source: Soup recipe
